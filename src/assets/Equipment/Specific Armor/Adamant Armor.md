Heavy [[Armor]]

This dull green armor is made of [[Adamant]] Metal, and is at the limits of what civilization is capable of creating without extensive use of magic. It is given only to the champions of a nation, the armor of a chosen one, a creation that makes the wearer seemingly invincible.
