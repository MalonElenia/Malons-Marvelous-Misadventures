Rogue

Masters of stealth, infiltration, and execution. The rogue is "that guy" someone knows who's the man for the job. The rogue is the guy who knows people higher up in town as much as those in the underbelly of society. Out-skill and outsmart your foes with the power of planning, tactics, and playing dirty.

Basic Statistics
HP +10/LV
ACC +1
Skills 8
Saves Dex
Mana 2+1/LV
Other +2 Stealth
+2 Luck Checks

1 - Techniques

You gain full progression in Scoundrel and Warfare.

1 - Ability

1 - Passive

1 - Sneak Attack

❗Surprise Attacks have +20 Crit Chance. In most cases, this means all Surprise attack hits are Critical Hits - you have a 100% critical hit rate with them.

1 - Critical Edge

Add another d10+LUK to your Critical Damage.

1 - Backstep

Once on your turn, during or after movement, you can move backwards on the path you took Elusive 10 ft without spending movement.

1 - Cunning Action

Once per round, gain an additional Standard Action or Swift Action that can't be used to Attack or cast a Spell. This must be a different action than you use with your regular standard or swift action.

1 - Dark Sight

Immediate Action, Recharge. Gain the ability to use Stealth anywhere and Hide on the same action, as well as cause all Accuracy checks against you to fail. Lasts five turns, or until you Attack, cast a Spell, or otherwise take an offensive action. Creatures that are not using Detect do not get additional checks to spot you.

1 - Haste

Standard Action, At-Will. Grant a touched ally +10 Speed and Jump for 10 minutes. Up to 3+CHA allies can benefit at the same time.

You gain this benefit passively.

2 - Evasion

You take half damage from Reflex save effects, and no damage when you pass the save.

2 - Rogue Talent

You gain a Rogue Talent every even level.

4 - Stroke of Luck

(1+LUK) times per day, you can force a specific serendipitous event as a free action. Advantageous scenarios could be something like a cart of hay to catch your fall, or a bird smashing into a window to get a guard to investigate. Things with a DC of 15 or lower happens automatically. Anything higher requires a roll.

6 - Uncanny Dodge

Stealth, Blindness, and Invisibility do not cause attacks to ❗Surprise you.

8 - Reliable Talent

If you Roll less than 10 on a Skill check, instead roll a 10.
