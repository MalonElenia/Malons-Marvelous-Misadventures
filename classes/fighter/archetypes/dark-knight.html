Dark Knight

Fighter Archetype.

Stand stalwart in the face of danger, an immovable obstacle that your opponents must fight tooth and nail to merely walk past, let alone survive. Outside of combat your massive skill bonuses can allow you to sense what others don't, and sniff out liars.

Based on the Maplestory class of the same name.

1 - Evil Eye Supernatural
You can manifest an ominous looking eye that floats around you, enhancing your senses and giving you a palpable aura of menace. You get Proficiency in Intimidation and Insight. You get a 4+½LV bonus to Perception, Insight and Intimidation. You also add this bonus against uses of Intimidation against you.

You can choose to make this eye disappear once per turn as a Free Action, but you lose the bonuses to Perception and Intimidation.

1 - No Escape
All effects that grant Elusive movement do not function against you while you wield a reach weapon, allowing you to make AoOs against a much larger number of creatures.

If a creature tries to move around you safely using Acrobatics while you wield a reach weapon, you add your Evil Eye bonus to your Reflex against them.

1 - Hyper Body
Passive. You get a +4 bonus to Fortitude. Your HP increases by 6+4/LV.

Standard Action, Limited. Buff. Grant 3+CHA allies an additional 4/LV HP. This is based on their level.
This bonus ends when they Long Rest, or you are Defeated.

2 - Stalwart
You take half damage from Fortitude Saves. When you pass a fort save, it deals no damage and has no additional effect.

4 - Polearm Mastery
You can make an unlimited number of AoOs, and they have +4 ACC.

6 - Placeholder

8 - Dark Impale Supernatural Concentration
Action-Attack, At-Will. Attack with a Pierce reach weapon. On hit, you leave a phantom copy of your weapon in the creature. The creature is unable to leave your natural reach and is Anchored. You can still use your weapon, but it is otherwise subject to Concentration rules, and the effect ends if you or the target are moved out of reach.

If you impale a second creature, the first is freed.
