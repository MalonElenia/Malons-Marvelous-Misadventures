Damage

Damage is a value that subtracts from a target's HP when the effect takes place.
"Losing HP" is synonymous with taking Damage.

Damage is always assigned a type. The physical damage types are Crush, Slash, and Pierce, and the elemental types are Magic, Lightning, Fire, Cold, Holy, Necrotic, and Psychic. These are also Tags.

If something deals multiple damage types, it usually adds both tags to the effect. For example, a flaming sword will deal Fire and Slash damage with its total damage, rather than separate d6 slash and d4 fire. Fire and Cold effects are mutually exclusive.

For Weapons, damage is determined by the weapon used and adds your STR. A Technique may add to this as well.
For Spell, this value is determined by your LV and SP.
Any given effect will describe how to determine the damage it deals.
