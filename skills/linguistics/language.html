Common Languages Common The default tongue - most intelligent creatures can speak this language with some level of proficiency. Conveniently, it's the same language the players speak at the table. Fiendish The language of Fiends. As a language created by creatures that seek only destruction, the language includes guttural sounds and subtle hums as a means of intimidation, and to better convey double meanings and secret messages. Celestial The language of Celestials. Originally used for holy invocations, oaths, and singing - making its native speakers easy to spot from how melodic their speech is. Sylvan The language of Fey. When nature speaks, this is the language it uses. Primordial The language of elementals and ancient common.

Morse Code A binary language that can turn any on/off state into characters of a language, whether it be through clicking or a smoke signal.

Bronze A unique language that can only be understood by the exceptionally unintelligent. To others, it sounds like nonsense Common. Any Humanoid with -1 INT can understand this language.
Druidic An alternative version of Sylvan druidic circles use that can be displayed in otherwise innocuous objects or markings in nature. Used to mark territory, hide secrets in plain sight, and perform rituals subtly.
Thieve's Cant A coded version of Common that thieves use to converse about malicious topics without alarming others around them.
