Named Bullet

Tier 4 Marksmanship Technique
1 Minute Cast Time, Limited
Supernatural

Effect
Range Reach
Target/Area Ammunition
Save -
Duration Until Rest

Imbue Ammunition with the conviction to defeat a specific enemy. You must know the specific individual's name, or else this ability has no effect.

This ammunition deals half damage to any target besides the specific individual. Against that individual, it gains +2 ACC and +d6 Critical Damage.
